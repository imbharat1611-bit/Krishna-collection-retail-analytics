# 🛍️ Krishna Collection Retail Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end retail analytics project built using **SQL, Excel, and Power BI** to analyze sales performance, customer trends, product insights, and business KPIs for a clothing retail store.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world retail business analytics workflow. It demonstrates data cleaning, SQL querying, dashboard creation, and business intelligence reporting using synthetic sales data inspired by a men's and children's clothing store.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **SQL Server** – Data analysis and business queries
- **Microsoft Excel** – Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Dashboard
- **Power BI** – Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard
- **Python** – Synthetic dataset generation
- **Git & GitHub** – Version control

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs

The dashboards include the following key business metrics:

- 💰 Total Revenue
- 📈 Total Profit
- 📦 Total Orders
- 🛒 Total Quantity Sold
- 💳 Revenue by Payment Mode
- 💵 Profit by Payment Mode
- 📅 Monthly Revenue Trend

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- UPI generated the highest revenue among all payment methods.
- Monthly revenue remained relatively stable with slight seasonal fluctuations.
- The business processed over **12,000 orders**.
- Total revenue exceeded **₹32 Million**.
- Interactive filters allow users to explore performance by payment mode.
